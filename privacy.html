Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

We built PRMSU Freedom Wall to let students post anonymously. This page explains what information may be collected and how it's used. We use only what's needed to run the platform.

What we collect What we don't ask Anonymous posts Reports Cookies Data safety Removal Contact
Please don't include private details in posts - no real name, student number, section, phone number, address, or anything you wouldn't want public. Once posted, others may have already copied it.

What we collect

  • The text of your post, its category, and note color
  • The date and time it was posted
  • A generated anonymous name (like ramonians-1234567) - not your identity
  • Likes and comments you make
  • If you report a post, that a report was made plus any reason you typed
  • If you apply to moderate: your name, email, and verification photos (see below)

Storage is handled by Supabase on our behalf. We don't run ads or tracking cookies to profile you.

What we don't ask for

We never ask for your school portal password, student number, payment info, full address, or government ID to post. Posting needs no account at all.

Anonymous posts

Posts are shown anonymously to the public. However, you're still responsible for what you submit, and harmful or illegal content is your responsibility.

Reports & moderation

Reports help moderators review posts that may break the rules. One report can't take a post down by itself, and one browser can report a post once.

Cookies & local storage

The wall keeps a few small things only in your own browser (never sent anywhere): your generated anonymous name, which posts you liked, and a short posting cooldown timer. When you report a post, a random one-time id from your browser is sent so the same browser can't report a post twice - it isn't tied to your identity.

Data safety & moderator applications

If you apply to moderate, we ask for a photo of your school ID and a selfie only to confirm you're a real Ramonian. These are stored privately, visible only to an admin, and permanently deleted the moment your application is approved or rejected. We never sell, share, or reuse them.

When content may be removed

Content that breaks our Community Rules may be hidden or deleted at our discretion. Because posts are anonymous we usually can't verify who wrote one - if you want something removed, use the Report button on that post or contact the creator.

Contact

Questions, or a request to remove something, can be directed to the creator, Red Pogi Lang. Interested in helping? Apply to moderate.

Unofficial student-made platform. Not affiliated with PRMSU. This policy may change; updates appear here with a new date.
