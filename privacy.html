Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The PRMSU Freedom Wall ("the wall") is a free, student-made space where anyone can post short anonymous messages that other people can read. Because a wall is a shared, public place, this is different from a private tool: what you post is stored on a server and shown to everyone. Please read this so you know exactly what happens to what you write.

1. Posts are public and anonymous

You do not need an account to post. We do not ask for your name, student number, or email. A post is published with either the optional nickname you type or simply "Anonymous". Anything you write in a post is visible to the public and can be read, screenshotted, or shared by anyone.

2. Please don't post personal or sensitive information

Do not include your full name, student number, phone number, address, passwords, or anyone else's private information. Do not post things you would not want seen publicly. Once something is posted publicly, other people may have already copied it even if it is later removed.

3. What we store

  • The text of your post, your chosen nickname (if any), and the note color.
  • The date and time the post was created.
  • If a post is reported, we store that a report was made and any reason text the reporter typed, so an admin can review it.

We do not knowingly store your IP address, device identifiers, or a profile about you. A short posting cooldown timer is kept only in your own browser. When you report a post, a random one-time id from your browser is sent so the same browser cannot report the same post twice; it is not tied to your identity.

4. Where it's stored

Posts, reports, and moderator applications are stored using Supabase, a third-party hosting and database provider, on our behalf. The site is hosted on a static web host (such as Netlify). These providers may process standard technical request information (such as an IP address) as part of delivering the site, according to their own privacy policies. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts.

5. Moderation

To keep the wall safe, administrators can hide or delete posts, and can read the reports submitted against a post. Reports are queued for an admin to review; a report never automatically hides or deletes anything on its own, and one person cannot take a post down by reporting it repeatedly. A word filter also runs in your browser to discourage offensive language before posting. Moderation is best-effort, and we cannot guarantee that every harmful post is caught or removed quickly.

6. Applying to be a moderator (ID and selfie)

Helping to moderate is optional. If you choose to apply, we ask for your full name, an email, a photo of your school ID, and a selfie, so an admin can confirm you are a real Ramonian. We treat this with care:

  • The ID photo and selfie are uploaded to private storage that only an admin can open. They are never shown on the public wall.
  • They are used only to verify your application.
  • They are permanently deleted the moment your application is approved or rejected. We do not keep your ID or your face after a decision is made.
  • We never sell, share, or use your identity for anything else.

If you are approved, the only thing we keep is the email you sign in with, so the system knows you are a moderator.

7. Removing a post

Because posts are anonymous, we usually cannot verify who wrote a given post. If you want a post removed, whether it is yours or one that harms you, use the Report button on that post, or contact the creator. We will remove content that violates our Community Rules at our discretion.

8. No tracking or ads

The wall does not run advertising and does not use analytics or tracking cookies to build a profile of you.

9. Children and students

This is a space intended for the PRMSU community. Post responsibly and follow the law and university conduct rules.

10. Not affiliated with PRMSU

This is an independent, unofficial project. It is not operated, endorsed, or affiliated with President Ramon Magsaysay State University. The university seal is used only to identify the school community the wall is made for.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with a new "last updated" date.

12. Contact

Questions, or a request to remove something, can be directed to the creator, Red Pogi Lang.
