Apply to help moderate

Want to help keep the wall safe? Send a quick application. The admin reviews each one and decides whether to approve you as a moderator. If approved, you sign in on the admin page with the email you give below.

Your identity stays safe. Your ID and selfie are used only to check that you are a real Ramonian. They are stored privately, are visible to the admin alone, and are permanently deleted the moment your application is approved or rejected. We never keep, use, or share your identity, and this info never appears anywhere on the public wall.
