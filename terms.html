Community Rules & Terms

Last updated: July 2, 2026

PRMSU Freedom Wall is a student-made anonymous space. Post freely, but keep it kind, safe, and respectful. By using the wall, you agree to these rules.

Quick rules Allowed Not allowed Moderation Anonymous Disclaimer Terms of use

Quick rules

No real names
No bullying
No doxxing
No threats
No explicit content
Keep it student-friendly

What's allowed

This is your space to be real. Post away:

ConfessionsCrush postsRants Funny campus storiesQuestionsLost & Found Campus tipsLooking for classmatesHarmless student experiences

What's not allowed

These get hidden or removed:

  • Real names without consent, student numbers, or sections used to target someone
  • Phone numbers, addresses, or personal photos without consent
  • Harassment, bullying, threats, or hate speech
  • Sexual harassment or explicit content
  • Fake accusations or posts that expose someone's private information
  • Impersonating PRMSU, faculty, staff, orgs, or student officers
  • Anything illegal under Philippine law

Moderation

Posts may be reviewed, hidden, or removed if they violate the rules. Reports are reviewed by student moderators, and repeated reports flag a post for review. Moderation is best-effort, so please report anything that crosses the line instead of replying with more of the same.

Anonymous posting

Posts show a random anonymous name, not your identity. But anonymous is not the same as no-rules: you are still responsible for what you submit. Don't post anything you wouldn't want screenshotted.

Disclaimer
