Community Rules & Terms

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The PRMSU Freedom Wall is a shared, anonymous space. "Anonymous" is not the same as "no rules." By posting here you agree to these rules and terms. If you do not agree, please don't post.

1. Be kind. The golden rule.

This wall is for venting, confessing, appreciating, and connecting, not for hurting people. Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. When in doubt, don't post it.

2. What is not allowed

  • Harassment or bullying of any specific person.
  • Hate speech or attacks based on gender, religion, ethnicity, disability, or sexual orientation.
  • Threats or incitement of violence or self-harm.
  • Doxxing: posting someone's private information (full name plus details, student number, address, phone, photos) without consent.
  • Sexual harassment or sexual content involving minors or non-consenting people.
  • Spam, scams, advertising, or repeated flooding of the wall.
  • Impersonation of another student, staff member, or the university.
  • Anything illegal under Philippine law.

3. Posts can be removed

Administrators may hide or delete any post at their discretion, especially content that breaks these rules or is reported by the community. Reported posts are placed in a review queue for an admin to check. Moderation is best-effort and may not be instant.

4. Report, don't retaliate

If you see something that breaks these rules, use the Report button instead of replying with more of the same. Reports go to the admins for review.

5. You are responsible for what you post

You are solely responsible for the content you submit. Do not post other people's private information, and do not post anything you don't have the right to share. Harmful, defamatory, or illegal posts are your responsibility, not the creator's.

6. Not an official university service

This is an independent, unofficial project. It is not created, operated, endorsed by, or affiliated with President Ramon Magsaysay State University. Views expressed by posters are their own and do not represent the university or the creator.

7. Provided "as is"

The wall is provided "as is" and "as available", without warranties of any kind. It may be unavailable, lose data, or contain content you find objectionable. To the maximum extent permitted by law, the creator is not liable for any damages, loss, or harm arising from use of the wall or from content posted by others.

8. Intellectual property

The President Ramon Magsaysay State University name and seal are the property of the university and are used here only to identify the community the wall serves. All other code and design of the wall belong to the creator.

9. Changes

These rules may be updated at any time. Continued use of the wall after changes means you accept the updated rules.

10. Contact

For questions, concerns, or removal requests, contact the creator, Red Pogi Lang.
